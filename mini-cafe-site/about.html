Our Story

Kapeng Matapang began as a small passion project among friends who wanted to bring out the bold flavors of Filipino coffee. From local farms to your cup, every sip is a tribute to strength, authenticity, and community.

Our Core Values

  • Strength: Coffee that fuels your ambitions.
  • Authenticity: Proudly sourced from local farmers.
  • Resilience: A brew that represents the Filipino spirit.
